# Verdant — product facts

A quick, accurate reference for the core product. Keep it in sync with the pricing table in
`CLAUDE.md` (the source of truth).

## The core promise

You point your camera at a plant and Verdant tells you what it is and exactly how to keep it
alive. Identification, a real care plan, and reminders that fit your home. No guesswork.

## Who it's for

People who love their plants but aren't sure what those plants need. First-time owners who just
brought something home, and growing collectors who want every plant to thrive. If you've ever
wondered "what is this, and why is it dying?", Verdant is for you.

## Tiers

| Tier | Price | Plants |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | Identify up to 5 plants a month, reminders for up to 3 plants, community access. |
| **Plus** | $5.99/mo ($39.99/yr) | Unlimited identification, unlimited care plans with smart seasonal reminders, photo diagnosis (Plant Doctor), light meter and room placement. |
| **Family** | $9.99/mo ($79.99/yr) | Everything in Plus, up to 5 members, a shared plant library, priority expert help, early access. |

Free gets you in the door with the magic moment. Plus is for a real collection: unlimited plants,
unlimited care. Family adds the whole household on top.
