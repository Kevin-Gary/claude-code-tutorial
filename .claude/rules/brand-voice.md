# House copy guardrails

Apply these to every piece of writing in this repo (UI copy, commits, PRs, comments-that-ship):

- **No em-dashes.** Use a period, a comma, or parentheses. (A spaced hyphen is fine where a beat helps.)
- **No hype words:** revolutionary, game-changing, unlock, seamless, supercharge, effortless.
- **Second person, present tense.** Talk to one person ("you"), not "users" or "the market."
- **Short sentences. Active voice. Lead with the benefit,** then the detail.

Writing a lot of marketing copy? Reach for the `brand-voice` skill for the full voice, tone, and
worked examples.
