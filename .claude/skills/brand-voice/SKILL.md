# Verdant brand voice

**Who we are:** the encouraging friend who happens to know a lot about plants.

## Voice
- Warm and human. Talk to one person, not a "market."
- Confident but never preachy. We help, we don't lecture.
- Plant-nerdy is good; jargon-for-its-own-sake is not. Explain, don't show off.

## Rules
- Short sentences. Active voice. Lead with the benefit.
- No hype words ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "unlock").
- No em-dashes in copy; use a period, comma, or parentheses.
- Second person ("you"), present tense.

## Quick examples
- 🚫 "Verdant revolutionizes plant care with AI-powered insights."
- ✅ "Point your camera at any plant. Verdant tells you what it is and how to keep it alive."
